Our Mission

To inspire girls to pick up an instrument regardless of experience, age, or background.

When we first started, most of us were very new to our instruments. Together we created a space to explore our musical expression and learn together. As an all girl band with a diverse background, we believe in inclusion within the music industry and hope to be an inspiration to others that it's never too late to get started no matter who you are.

Based in Seattle, Washington, The Daughters of Venus are five University of Washington students bringing a fresh, feminine sound to alternative rock.

Music That Moves You.

The Daughters of Venus believe in the power of music to connect, heal, and inspire. Their lyrics explore themes of love, identity, and the struggles of modern life.

Our Values

Authenticity

We believe in staying true to ourselves and our music. Every song we create is a reflection of our experiences, emotions, and the world around us. Authenticity is at the heart of everything we do.

Connection

Music is a universal language that brings people together. We strive to create songs that resonate with our listeners, fostering a sense of connection and community.

Empowerment

We aim to empower our listeners through our music. Whether it's through lyrics that inspire or melodies that uplift, we want our songs to be a source of strength and positivity.
