DAUGHTERS OF VENUS

Seattle's Rising Aternative Rock Band

Based in Seattle, Washington, The Daughters of Venus are five University of Washington students bringing a fresh sound to alternative rock. Their music is raw, emotional, and unapologetically authentic.

Music That Moves You.

The Daughters of Venus believe in the power of music to connect, heal, and inspire. Their lyrics explore themes of love, identity, and the struggles of modern life, resonating with a diverse audience.

Who Are The Daughters of Venus?

Meet the Band

Five talented University of Washington students came together to form The Daughters of Venus, blending their unique voices and styles into a cohesive sound. Their mission revolves around creating representation for female and POC musicians in the Seattle music scene. They promote their music through several online platforms that help them connect with their audience and inspire others to pick up an instrument regardless of their gender or background!

Alternative Rock

Their music is a fusion of gritty guitar riffs, haunting melodies, and powerful lyrics that feature the voices of their guitar and bass players.

Seattle Roots

Inspired by the rich musical history of Seattle, The Daughters of Venus are proud to carry on the legacy of alternative rock in the Pacific Northwest.

Live Performances

Known for their electrifying live shows, the band brings energy and passion to every performance, leaving audiences wanting more.

Upcoming Projects
